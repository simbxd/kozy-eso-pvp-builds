## Overview

This build is designed for players who want to control engagements — you choose when to fight and when to disappear. The core loop is: open from stealth, burst with a full combo, reenter stealth if the target survives.

## Why This Morph Choice

**Surprise Attack** over Veiled Strike because the Major Fracture application is more valuable in PvP than the stun in most scenarios — your target will break free immediately, but the armor reduction stays.

**Incapacitating Strike** over Soul Harvest for the Major Defile on the execute window. Denying healing at low health is often the deciding factor in a kill.

## Rotation

1. Enter stealth → Shadowy Disguise for guaranteed crit
2. Surprise Attack → Killer Blade (if below 25%)
3. Incapacitating Strike
4. Reenter cloak with Shadowy Disguise or natural stealth

Keep Siphoning Strikes and Leeching Strikes active on bar 2 for sustain between engagements.
